# Lemon Orzo Soup

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 large onion
- 2 cloves garlic, minced (2 tsp)
- 2 celery stalks, sliced crosswise 1/2 inch
- 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
- Kosher salt
- Ground black pepper
- 1 tsp dried parsley
- 1/2 tsp dried thyme
- 1/2 tsp dried rosemary
- 1/2 tsp ground ginger
- 8 cups chicken stock (or broth)
- 1 pound roasted or rotisserie chicken, pulled into chunks
- 4 tablespoons lemon juice
- 3/4 cup uncooked orzo pasta

## Utensils

- Large pot with lid
- Ladle / large wooden spoon
- Silicone spatula

## Directions

1. In large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat and add
2. Add onion, garlic, celery, carrots, couple pinches of salt, and pepper
3. Push everything to side of pot, add thyme, rosemary, ginger, and stir herbs to toast for ~30 seconds
4. Stir herbs into vegetables
5. Add stock, chicken, and lemon juice
6. Bring to simmer, then cover and let simmer ~5 minutes
7. Add orzo, cover and let simmer until al dente, usually ~8 minutes
8. Optional: Garnish with fresh chopper parsley
